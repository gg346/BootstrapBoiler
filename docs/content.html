Katie Hafner

Born in 1957, Katie Hafner is an American journalist from Rochester, NY. She writes books and articles especially about technology, health care and society. Some of her work includes "Cyberpunk: Outlaws and Hackers on the Computer Frontier", "The House at the Bridge: A Story of Modern Germany", and most importantly, "Where Wizards Stay Up Late: The Origins of the Internet", with co-writer and husband, Matthew Lyon.

Matthew Lyon

Born on May 21, 1956, in Connecticut, and died from cardiac arrest on February 16, 2002, Matthew Lyon was the vice chancellor for public affairs at the University of California, Berkeley. He also had background in journalism and politics in which he used to lead a professional team to define and communicate the goals of a diverse campus in strategical ways. Lyon is survived by his wife and their daughter Zoe.
